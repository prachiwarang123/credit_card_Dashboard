# 💳 Credit Card Financial Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

🚀 This repository showcases a comprehensive **Credit Card Financial Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Power BI**, designed to transform raw financial and customer data into actionable business insights. The dashboard provides a complete overview of customer behavior, spending patterns, transaction activities, and revenue performance, enabling organizations to make informed, data-driven decisions.

By leveraging interactive visualizations and dynamic filtering capabilities, this project helps stakeholders identify high-value customer segments, analyze transaction trends, monitor financial performance, and uncover growth opportunities within the credit card business ecosystem.

## 👥 Credit Card Customer Report

The **Customer Report Dashboard** focuses on customer demographics and revenue contribution analysis. It helps businesses understand who their customers are and how different customer segments impact overall revenue generation.

### 📊 Key Features

✅ Revenue analysis by Income Groups (High, Medium, Low)
✅ Customer segmentation based on occupation:

* Businessman
* White-Collar Employees
* Government Employees
* Self-Employed Professionals
* Blue-Collar Workers
* Retirees

✅ Revenue distribution across different Age Groups
✅ Gender-based revenue comparison
✅ Marital Status analysis and spending behavior
✅ Education Level performance insights
✅ Top-performing States based on revenue generation
✅ Dependency-based customer analysis
✅ Weekly Revenue Trend monitoring throughout the year
✅ Card Category performance comparison:

* Blue
* Silver
* Gold
* Platinum

### 🎯 Business Benefits

* Identify the most profitable customer segments.
* Understand customer demographics and spending behavior.
* Improve customer targeting and marketing strategies.
* Enhance customer acquisition and retention efforts.
* Support strategic business decision-making through data insights.

## 🎯 Project Objective

The primary objective of this project is to demonstrate how **Business Intelligence (BI)** and **Data Analytics** can be used to convert complex credit card customer and transaction datasets into meaningful insights. The dashboards empower organizations to monitor business performance, identify trends, optimize strategies, and drive sustainable growth through data-driven decision-making.

📊 **Tools Used:** Power BI, Data Modeling, DAX, Data Visualization, Business Analytics

🚀 **Outcome:** A complete end-to-end financial analytics solution that delivers actionable insights into customer behavior, transaction activity, and revenue performance.
